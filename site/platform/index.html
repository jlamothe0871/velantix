Axiom V1 — Decision Intelligence Engine

Upload. Process. Blueprint. Deliver.

Axiom is not a monitoring tool. Not a migration executor. It's the intelligence layer that tells you exactly what to do — and why — before a single workload moves.

01

Create Assessment & Upload Data

Create a new project, name the customer environment, and upload your RVTools CSV export. Axiom validates required columns, data completeness, and flags anomalies before processing begins.

02

Processing Pipeline Runs

Axiom runs six sequential stages: data normalization, classification engine, cost model, timeline engine, tool recommendation, and AI narrative generation. 1,000–5,000 VMs processed in under 5 minutes.

03

Review & Approve Classifications

Every VM receives a classification (Decommission, Rehost, Containerize, or Complex) with a confidence score and reason code. Review edge cases, adjust classifications, and approve the output before generation.

04

Generate & Deliver Outputs

Download the executive PDF, technical dataset, and optional slide summary. Email directly to customer stakeholders. Every deliverable is ready for executive presentation or immediate partner handoff.

VM Classification Engine

Every VM. A clear recommendation.

Axiom classifies each workload into one of four action categories — with confidence scores and reason codes for every decision.

🗑️

Decommission

CPU <5%, Memory <10%, idle or powered off. Eliminate waste and reduce licensing cost immediately.

🔁

Rehost

Stable workload, moderate utilization, traditional OS. Lift-and-shift to KubeVirt or target hypervisor.

📦

Containerize

Linux workloads, API-driven or scalable, variable load patterns. Move to containers for operational efficiency.

⚠️

Complex

High CPU, large memory, legacy systems. Requires dedicated planning, expert guidance, and phased approach.

AI Integration — Strict Boundary

AI that amplifies data.
Not invents it.

Velantix Axiom uses AI precisely — for narrative generation, insight summarization, and executive communication. The numbers come from your data. AI never calculates costs, classifies workloads without deterministic logic, or generates figures without a data foundation.

✓
AI generates: Executive narratives, insight summaries, risk statements, recommendations
✗
AI does not: Calculate costs, classify workloads (primary logic), generate numbers without real data
