Our Mission

Make the right move undeniable.

Infrastructure transformation shouldn't require a leap of faith. It should be driven by data, modeled with precision, and executed with confidence. Velantix Axiom exists to make that possible — for every organization, at every scale.

Our Story

Thirty years of transformations.
One platform to systematize them.

Velantix was founded in Nolanville, Texas in April 2026 by a U.S. Army veteran and enterprise infrastructure architect with over 30 years of experience across EMC, Compellent, WWT, and Pure Storage.

In that time, the founder led more than 1,500 enterprise migrations, contributed to over $90M in services revenue, and built transformation programs for some of the largest organizations in the world.

The pattern was always the same: customers knew they needed to transform — they just didn't have a clear, data-driven picture of what that meant for their specific environment. Velantix Axiom is the answer to that gap.
