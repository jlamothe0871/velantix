Common Questions

Before you reach out.

What data do I need to get started? +
All you need is an RVTools CSV export from your VMware environment. RVTools is a free utility available at robware.net. Optional enrichment data (IOPS, network usage, application tags) can improve classification accuracy but is not required for a full assessment.
How long does an assessment take? +
Axiom processes 1,000–5,000 VMs in under 5 minutes. The full pipeline — normalization, classification, cost modeling, timeline, and AI narrative generation — runs automatically. You receive the executive PDF and technical dataset the same session.
Is my infrastructure data secure? +
Yes. Velantix processes your data with encrypted storage and strict API key protection. We do not retain customer infrastructure data after assessment completion. An on-premise deployment option is available for federal and security-sensitive environments.
Is Axiom vendor-neutral? +
100%. Axiom's recommendations are driven entirely by your infrastructure data — not by vendor relationships or preferred platforms. We support all major VMware exit destinations including OpenShift, Nutanix, Proxmox, and all major hyperscalers. You choose where you're going. We show you the optimal path.
What does the output look like? +
Every assessment produces: (1) an executive PDF with AI-generated summary, risk analysis, TCO model, and transformation blueprint; (2) a full technical dataset with per-VM classification, confidence scores, and reason codes; and (3) an optional slide summary deck for stakeholder presentation.
Can partners brand the output? +
Yes. Certified Partner tier and above includes co-branded output options. OEM/Strategic partners have access to full white-label deliverables. Contact us to discuss partner program options.
